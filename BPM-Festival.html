    The BPM Festival is a global music festival dedicated to electronic dance music (EDM) and techno. The festival originated in Playa del Carmen, Mexico, in 2008, and it has since expanded to various locations around the world. It's known for featuring top-notch DJs and artists in the EDM and techno scenes, creating an immersive experience for electronic music enthusiasts. The festival typically includes day and night events, beach parties, and showcases at various venues, making it a vibrant celebration of electronic music culture.
