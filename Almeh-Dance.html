    Whenever someone thinks of "exotic dances" they most likely think of the image of a belly dancer. With their sensual movements, intricate rhythms and elaborate costumes, belly dancers have captivated audiences around the world. But belly dance is not just sensual lines and colorful costumes. It is an ancient dance with a rich history dating back hundreds of years. Mixing the cultures of Morocco and Middle East, headdresses with trays and candles have been recreated to make these costumes more interesting and original.
